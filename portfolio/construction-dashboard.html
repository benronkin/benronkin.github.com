Reports and dashboards for construction crews

Google Apps Script

SUMMARY

The client needed automation to calculate commissions for construction crews. A Google Form is used to capture work details. A Google Apps Script calculates commissions. Google Sheets reports and dashboards visualize the data for analysis and insights.

Capture work details

The client uses a Google Form to capture information on a new job. Every form entry includes general job information, as well as daily data capture of the technicians performing the work.

When the form is submitted, its responses are captured in a Google Sheet that my Google Apps Script automation can access.

Configurable settings

The Google Apps Script operates inside a Google Sheet where using a Settings tab, the client can change various inputs such as split rates, deduction rates, etc.

The script automation runs on all form submissions – both historical and new – enabling the client to perform what-if scenario analyses by changing the settings and rerunning the automation.

Commission calculation

The Google Apps Script uses the client settings and the form submissions in order to calculate payouts for every crew member on every job. The calculation are quite complex: for instance, the crew commission depends on the number of full-time and part-time members, which can vary on different days of the job.

The automation uses sophisticated business logic to calculate commissions correctly.

Consolidated Reports

The client's Google Sheet contains a report tab that consolidates multiple crew member rows to a single row per job. Sophisticated formulas find the right data item inside the automation's raw data tab and present it in a consolidated view.

The client can easily see how much commission every crew member was paid per job.

Executive Dashboard

The client's Google Sheet contains an interactive dashboard that enables managers to keep track of key business metrics. The dashboard is connected directly to the raw data from the automation, giving a bird's-eye view of crew commissions, job completions, and more.

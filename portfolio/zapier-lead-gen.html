Automate lead generation with Zapier

Zapier

SUMMARY

The client needed to automate the processing of new sales leads. I built a Zapier automation that adds leads to a CRM, sends an automated text to the lead, and adds the lead to a lead nurturing campaign.

Capture lead info from Calendly bookings

A Zap is configured to monitor the client's Calendly account for new meeting bookings, looking for an "Invitee Created" event. When a booking occurs, the Zap captures the lead's name, email, phone number, and notes from the submission.

Configurable settings

The Google Apps Script operates inside a Google Sheet where using a Settings tab, the client can change various inputs such as split rates, deduction rates, etc.

The script automation runs on all form submissions – both historical and new – enabling the client to perform what-if scenario analyses by changing the settings and rerunning the automation.

Store lead information in HubSpot CRM

All the above Zaps are set with an action to store the lead information in HubSpot CRM, mapping the different input fields to the fields in the CRM. The Zap first looks up the lead's email address in HubSpot. If it doesn't find a contact with that email it instructs HubSpot to create a new contact.

Sync HubSpot CRM to Google Sheets

The client needed to share some of the HubSpot data with an outside agency, but did not want to provide them access to the system. Therefore, I developed a Zap that synchs new contacts in HubSpot directly to a Google Sheet that the agency could access.

Add lead to ActiveCampaign

The new lead is also automatically added to ActiveCampaign as a new contact, and is assigned to a lead nurturing campaign.

Text the lead via Twilio SMS

As soon as the lead is added to HubSpot, a multi-step Zap uses the lead's first name and phone number to send a confirmation text message to the lead using Twilio.

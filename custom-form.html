Custom form to Google sheet

Google Workspace

Summary

I needed a contact form deployed for this portfolio website. Opted to have a custom form that fits my site design instead of using Google Form. I built a custom form that, when submitted, sends the form data to a Google Apps Script that stores submissions in a Google Sheet and sends me an email notification.

Custom contact form

Developed a custom HTML form that fits the look and feel of my portfolio website.

Form looks much better than a standard Google Form.

Store submissions in a Google Sheet

The form sends every submission to a Google Apps Scripts that validates the information passed in. The script then stores the submission in a dedicated Google Sheet that is easy to access.

Real-time email notifications

My Google Apps Script bundles the form submission into an email message, which it sends to me in real-time.

This enables me to respond quickly to every contact.

Expand Google Sheet rows

Google Apps

  • Key highlights

  • Client needed to expand Google Sheet rows based on custom rules
  • I implemented Google Apps Script to duplicate rows based on date range in row
  • The script runs on every row that the client marks for expansion

Summary

In this small project, the client had a Google Sheet with two tabs (summary and detail), and required a way to expand summary rows based on date range in each of the rows. I developed a Google Apps Script to enable the client to expand the rows on demand.

Expandable summary rows

The Summary tab includes campaign rows with start and end dates. The client wanted a way to indicate which rows to expand using a "Split Campaign" column.

Here we see two rows marked for expansion: the first is for dates between Feb and May, and other for the month of March.

Detail rows for each month

Once the user flagged the two rows for expansion, my Google Apps Script expanded them on the Details tab based on their data ranges. Each row is marked by the month.

The first campaign received four details row for the months Feb through May, and the second campaign received one details row for the month of March.

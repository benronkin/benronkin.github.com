Lead sharing app

Google Cloud

  • Key highlights

  • An integrated Google Cloud + Google Apps solution
  • Automatically emailing channel partners links to their lead files
  • Partners access their leads via the link (authentication required)
  • Ingesting source data from two Google Sheets
  • Grouping sales leads by assigned partner IDs
  • Creating separate CSV files for each registered partner
  • Assigning reader privilege to each partner email address
  • Tracking lead distribution status
  • Cron jobs trigger the system every few hours

Summary

The client needed to share sales leads with channel partners in a secured way. I implemented a solution that ingests sales leads and assigned partners, builds CSV files on Google Cloud Storage, and routinely emails partners links to the CSV files. Partners must authenticate into their Google account to download the file.

Date ingestion

Sales leads are provided to the system via a Google Sheet on a routine basis. Each lead is assigned to a partner ID.

Partner IDs and their respective email addresses are provided to the system via a separate Google Sheet.

The client didn't want to manage hundreds of Google Sheets for each of its partners. A more robust solution was created.

Automated data processing

To handle the massive amount of sales leads and channel partners, I created Cloud Functions on Google Cloud Platform.

The functions are triggered via Cloud Scheduler that publishes messages to Pub/Sub topics. The functions separate leads by campaign and partner ID.

Data persistence

The system stores every group of partner leads in individual CSV files, which it stores in a Google Cloud Storage bucket.

To ensure data security, the system assigns Reader privilege to the partner email addresses that are supplied to the system. Partners must authenticate using this email address to access the file.

Email notifications

The system generates and sends emails to every channel partner with a link to the file. Partners must authenticate to access the file.

Email tracking

The system reports on the status of every channel partner email address, whenever email addresses are added, and when emails are sent.

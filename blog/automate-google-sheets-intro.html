Automate Google Sheets: A blog post series

today April 13, 2022

Having collaborated with many clients on their Google Sheets automation needs, I've noticed several patterns emerging in how business users seek to improve their productivity with tabulated data.

To share what I've learned, I decided to document these patterns in a blog post series, hoping that it might give business managers ideas on how to become more productive with their spreadsheet use.

The topics included in this blog post series are:

  • Importing data from Google Drive files
  • Merging multiple data sources into one sheet
  • Executing mail (or document) merge automatically
  • Automating the export of data from a spreadsheet to different destinations
  • Creating or otherwise manipulating spreadsheets based on business rules
  • Summarizing information and keeping track of spreadsheet data

I'll update this post with links to the new posts as I publish them. I plan to add relevant case studies to each post that I hope will clarify the points in the post.

Banner Engine: Privacy Policy

Ben Ronkin ("the Developer") provides the Banner Engine Add-on for Google Workspace ("the Add-on") and the website at benronkin.com (referred to hereafter as "the Website") that provide the service.

This page is used to inform Add-on users and Website visitors regarding the Developer's policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personally Identifiable Information and Non-Personally Identifiable Information if anyone decided to use the Add-on or the Website.

If you choose to use the Add-on or the Website, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. The Personally Identifiable Information and Non-Personally Identifiable Information that the Developer collects are used for providing and improving the Service. The Developer will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Developer collects your email address when you purchase the license for the Add-on. The email address is used to send you an email receipt and to provide you with customer service. Your email address will not be used in any other way.

The Website collects certain Non-Personally Identifiable Information. The Developer uses your IP address to diagnose problems with the Website, to administer the Website, and to gather demographic information. The Website analytic solution will also provide the Developer with summary, but not individual, reports that will tell the Developer how visitors access and use the Website.

Cookies

The Website implements third-party analytics solution that may place cookies on your computer. Cookies placed on your computer by third parties by or through the Website, if any, are not the responsibility of the Developer and are not subject to this Privacy Policy. Please contact the third party placing these cookies to find out what information is collected and how it is used. You can usually edit your browser not to accept cookies, but the features of our Web Site may not function properly if you do accept cookies.

Google Drive Access

Information about managing, along with removing third-party access to Google Drive can be found on the Google help page Third-party sites & apps with access to your account.

Google API Services User Data Policy

The Developer's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Security

The Developer values your trust in providing him your Personal Information, thus he is striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and the Developer cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Links to Other Sites

The Add-on or the Website may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by the Developer. Therefore, the Developer strongly advises you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. The Developer has no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

The Add-on or the Website does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Developer does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. In the case that the Developer discovers that a child under 13 has provided the Developer with personal information, the Developer immediately deletes this from his servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided the Developer with personal information, please contact the Developer so that he will be able to do necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer reserves the right to update this privacy policy with or without notice. Thus, the Developer advises you to review this page periodically for any changes. The Developer will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of the Add-on or the Web Site or your dealings with the Add-ons or the Web Site, you can contact the Developer here: https://benronkin.com/contact

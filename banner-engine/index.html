Banner Engine for Google Sheets

Create web banners automatically by injecting text from Google Sheets into your Google Slides and exporting them as images.

InstallUpgrade

Easy to set up

Once you purchase Banner Engine, you will receive an email message with links to a Google Sheet and a Google Slide. The links will enable you to copy these two files.

In your Google Drive, you place the Google slides in a Templates folder. You then create a second folder to house your output banners.

Customizable layouts

Inside the Google Slide file, you define the layouts. Each slide is a banner layout. You add your static images and icons to the slide.

You also add one or more textholders that will be replaced with your actual text. In the slide note, you enter the name of the layout.

You can create as many layout slides as you want. You can also duplicate the Google Slide file and have different files for different aspect ratios.

Command center

Use the Google Sheet to manage the creation of banners.

Each row in the sheet represents a banner image. Banner Engine goes through your templates folder and collects the names of the files and the names of their layouts automatically, so all you need to do is pick up your layouts from a list.

Enter your desired file name, as well as the actual text that will be inserted into the placeholders. You can have as many placeholders as you want, and you don't have to use all of them in every layout.

Your banners are ready

Click the custom menu option to start Banner Engine. It will go through every row of the sheet and create an image for it. The image will be saved in your output folder, and its URL will appear in the Google Sheet.

You can simply select the images in your Google Drive folder and download them to your computer.
